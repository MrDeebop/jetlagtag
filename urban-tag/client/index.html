HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Finish the game with the most coins. Every player takes a turn as the Runner — the person trying to reach their goal location without getting tagged.

🏃 THE RUNNER

You have a goal location shown on the Goals Map. Navigate to it without being physically tagged by a Chaser. Complete challenges along the way to earn bonus coins. You start each turn with a 50 🪙 bonus on top of your existing balance.

🕵 THE CHASERS

Find and physically tag the Runner. Use the live tracker map to see their GPS location in real time. Once you tag them, confirm it in the app — the Runner's turn ends and the next player becomes the Runner. Chasers do not spend coins on transport.

🔄 TURN ROTATION

Players rotate as Runner in the order they were entered at the start. When all players have had a turn as Runner, the game ends and final scores are revealed.

🚌 TRANSPORT (RUNNER ONLY)

Only the Runner pays for transport. Walk is free. Bike costs 2 🪙/min, Bus 5 🪙/min, Train 10 🪙/min, Taxi 15 🪙/min. Always stop the timer when you exit or you'll keep getting charged.

⛓ JAIL

After tagging the Runner, all Chasers go to Jail for 5 minutes. Stay within 100 ft of the tag location. Moving away triggers a warning to all players. You cannot tag anyone while jailed.

📋 CHALLENGES

The Runner receives challenges like "find a mural" or "cross a bridge." Complete them to earn between 10–30 🪙. Veto a challenge to skip it, but you'll be locked out of transport and new challenges for 5 minutes. All players start with 100 🪙.

🛒 SHOP (RUNNER ONLY)

Spend coins on power-ups: ⚡ Double Value + Double Skip Penalty (25 🪙), 📡 Location Ping — see all chasers' positions (100 🪙), 🚫 Tracker Off for 10 min (150 🪙), 🧊 Chasers Stand Still for 10 min (200 🪙).

⚠️ IMPORTANT RULES

  • Tagging must be physical — you must actually touch the Runner.
  • Goal locations are visible to everyone on the Goals Map — no hiding.
  • During Jail, Chasers must genuinely stay within 100 ft of the tag spot.
  • During Chasers Stand Still, Chasers must not move — honour system.
  • All transport costs are deducted from the Runner's coins only.
  • If you run out of coins you can still play, but you can't use paid transport or the shop.
  • The Gamemaster sets goal locations before the game — players should not look at that screen.
